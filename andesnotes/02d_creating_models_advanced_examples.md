# ANDES Study Notes — Advanced Model Examples (BusFreq / TGOV1 / IEEEST)

Sources:
- example-dynamic
- example-tgov1
- example-ieeest

## BusFreq
- Dynamic measurement-style model
- Reads bus states and estimates local frequency
- Participates in dynamic simulation, not power injection in power flow

## TGOV1
- Turbine-governor dynamics and deadband-related behavior
- Useful for frequency response sensitivity studies

## IEEEST
- Stabilizer-oriented control example
- Highlights signal path, gain, and phase shaping interactions

## Engineering Use
These examples are strong templates for extending control and measurement models while keeping reproducible validation paths.
